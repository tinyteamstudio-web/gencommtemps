How GEN-02 Is Structured

GEN-02 organizes history by groups or branches. Each family, clan, or unit has its own section, while remaining part of one unified archive.

This allows future families or members to be added without breaking the original structure.

Preferred Output Formats

  • A4 or A3 registry-style layout
  • Volume or chapter-based book format
  • Expandable digital structure

GEN-02 is recommended for A3 or Book formats due to scale.
