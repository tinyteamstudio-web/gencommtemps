How GEN-02 Is Structured

GEN-02 organizes history by groups or branches. Each family, clan branch, or unit has its own section, while remaining part of one unified archive.

This makes it easy to add new members later without breaking the original structure.

Preferred Output Formats

  • A4 or A3 registry-style layout (easy reference)
  • Book Format volume or chapter-based structure (best for very large groups)
  • Expandable digital structure for ongoing updates

Due to scale, GEN-02 is commonly delivered in A3 or Book format.
