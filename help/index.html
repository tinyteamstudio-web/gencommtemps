Handling Complex Family Structures

Families come in many forms. The TinyTeam Digital Library is designed to support real life, not force families into rigid templates.

Blended families (multiple marriages or partners)

Each relationship can be clearly recorded. Parents, step-parents, and children are shown in a way that respects their connections without confusion.

Same father or mother, different partners

Children can be linked correctly to their biological parents while still showing household or partnership relationships where appropriate.

Adoption (formal or informal)

Adopted children can be represented with dignity. Notes can be added to explain the relationship in a respectful, private way.

Unknown or undisclosed parents

It is acceptable to leave certain fields blank or mark them as unknown. The system respects privacy and sensitive histories.

How Delivery Works

TinyTeam Digital Library uses a manual, human-reviewed process.

  1. You choose a product and complete payment
  2. You email your receipt and basic details
  3. Your digital library is carefully prepared
  4. You receive access or files via email

This approach ensures accuracy, privacy, and long-term reliability.
