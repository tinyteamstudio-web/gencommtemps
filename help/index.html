Handling Complex Family Structures

Families come in many forms. The TinyTeam Digital Library is designed to reflect real life â€” not force families into rigid templates.

Blended families (multiple marriages or partners)

Same father or mother, different partners

Children can be linked correctly to their biological parents while still showing household or partnership relationships where appropriate.

Adoption (formal or informal)

Adopted children can be represented with dignity. Notes may be added to explain the relationship in a respectful and private way.

Unknown or undisclosed parents

How Delivery Works

  2. You email your receipt and basic details
  3. Your digital library is carefully prepared
  4. You receive access or files via email

This approach ensures accuracy, privacy, and long-term reliability.
