Living Updates

Your library is designed to grow with your family or community. Updates are handled manually and human-reviewed so details stay accurate and respectful.

What you can request

  • Add a new person / member
  • Correct spelling or display name
  • Update relationships (parent/spouse/guardian)
  • Add or update an “In Loving Memory” tribute
  • Replace or remove a photo (privacy)

No pressure. Share what you know, and we’ll guide you if clarification is needed.

Download update templates

These make your update request simple and consistent.

1) General Update Request

Corrections, memorial notes, privacy changes, and other requests.

Download update-request.txt

2) Add Member List

Add one or many people using a simple CSV list.

Download add-member.csv

How to send your update

  1. Download and fill out one (or both) templates
  2. Attach photos (optional)
  3. Email everything to smallsteps@tinyteamstudio.online

Subject example: Library Update – (Family/Community Name)
