Living Updates

Your library is meant to grow gently over time. Every update is human-reviewed (no automation) to keep names, relationships, and tributes accurate, respectful, and safe.

What “Living Updates” means

Life changes—new family members, corrected spellings, private details that need protection, or a tribute that deserves careful wording. Living Updates is our calm way of keeping your library true and current.

Email-based only Manual verification Privacy-respectful Heirloom-focused

Updates you can request

Add / include someone

Add a new person or member, or connect someone to the right household/branch.

Corrections

Spelling, display name, dates, labels, or small details that need to be fixed.

Relationship edits

Parent, spouse/partner, guardian, sibling links—handled carefully and clearly.

Memorial / tribute updates

Add or refine “In Loving Memory” notes, dates, and respectful wording.

Privacy changes

Remove/replace a photo, hide a detail, or request a safer display format.

General refinement

Anything that improves clarity, dignity, and accuracy—just describe what you want.

No pressure. Share what you know. If anything is unclear, we’ll reply with gentle questions.

How to send your update (email only)

  1. Write what you want to update (simple words are fine).
  2. If you have photos you want changed, attach them (optional).
  3. Email your request to smallsteps@tinyteamstudio.online.

Use this subject format: Library Update – (Family/Community Name)

Copy-paste email format (recommended)

You can copy this into your email so we can process your update faster:

Library Name: (Family/Community Name)
Page / Person: (Who or where this update applies)
What to change: (Old detail → New detail)
Relationship details: (If applicable: parent/spouse/guardian links)
Notes: (Any context, preferred wording, privacy requests)
Attachments: (Optional: photos to add/replace/remove)

Email TinyTeam Open Help Guide

We do not accept uploads through the website. Everything is handled through email to keep the system safe and simple.

Review & respect

Because this is a heritage-focused library, we may confirm sensitive changes before publishing—especially for relationship edits, memorial notes, or privacy removals. This protects families, communities, and the integrity of the record.
