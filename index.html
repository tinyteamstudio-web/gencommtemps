What Is TinyTeam Digital Library?

Think of TinyTeam like a calm, organized family archive — where names, relationships, and life details can be recorded gently and clearly.

It’s made for mixed generations: elders who prefer simple instructions, and younger family members who can help keep the library updated over time.

From Living Records to Lasting Memorabilia

What starts as simple family information can grow into something meaningful: a clean hierarchy, a clan record, a community directory, or a printed book that future generations can understand and keep.

You can keep it digital, print it now, or print it later — your library stays ready.

Who This Is For

  • Families and extended clans
  • Communities and organizations
  • Alumni groups and associations
  • Anyone preserving people and connections over time

If your family setup is complicated (blended families, adoption, multiple parents), TinyTeam is designed to respect real life and keep things understandable.

Inside Your Digital Library

  • People and relationship records (easy to read)
  • Family, group, or organization structures
  • Optional Legacy Pages (memories, aspirations, tributes, photos)
  • Print-ready outputs (A5, A4, A3, book formats)
  • Cloud-style access you can return to anytime

Your library is designed to grow — you can start small, then expand over time.
