TinyTeam Digital Library — FAQ

Real answers for real families, communities, and organizations.

What if a person has different mothers or fathers?

TinyTeam supports real-life family structures. A person can be connected through multiple parental or guardian relationships. Nothing is forced into a single pattern.

Are blended families, step-parents, or adopted children supported?

Yes. Relationships are defined by connection, not labels. You decide how people are linked and described.

How do we mark loved ones who have passed away?

You may gently mark a person as In Loving Memory. Their place in the family or community remains visible, respected, and intact.

Why are packages based on the number of people?

All packages include the same features. Capacity simply ensures the system stays organized and comfortable as your family or community grows.

What happens if we exceed our package limit?

Nothing is deleted. You can upgrade or add capacity anytime. Your data remains safe.

Why is this better than documents or folders?

Documents store information. TinyTeam preserves relationships, structure, and meaning — something folders can’t show.

What are Legacy Pages?

Legacy Pages let you write aspirations, stories, and tributes, and attach photos or memorabilia. They can stay separate or be merged later into your main library.

If we print a hardbound version, is formatting automatic?

Yes. Page layout, margins, and binding rules are handled automatically so you don’t need to worry about technical details.
