What Is TinyTeam Digital Library?

TinyTeam is a digital library designed to help families, communities, and organizations organize people and relationships in a way that feels respectful, flexible, and future-ready.

It is not just a place to store information — it’s a space where connections, histories, and stories can live together and evolve over time.

From Living Data to Lasting Memorabilia

What begins as simple information can become something meaningful — a family hierarchy, a clan record, a community directory, or a printed book that future generations can hold, read, and understand.

Who This Is For

  • Families and extended clans
  • Communities and organizations
  • Alumni groups and associations
  • Anyone who wants to preserve people and connections over time

Inside Your Digital Library

  • People and relationship records
  • Families, groups, or organizational structures
  • Optional Legacy Pages for memories and aspirations
  • Print-ready outputs (A5, A4, A3, book formats)
  • Cloud-based access that grows with you
