Step 1: Name Your Library

Choose a clear name your family or community will recognize. Simple is best. This name will be used on your library pages.

What to decide

  • Library Name (example: “Dela Cruz Family” or “Barangay San Isidro Community”)
  • Product Code: GEN-01 (Family) or GEN-02 (Community/Clan)

Helpful tip: if there are multiple branches, you can still start with one shared library name and we’ll organize it cleanly.

No forms No uploads Email submission Human-reviewed

Copy this into your notes so Step 4 is easy:
