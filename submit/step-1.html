Step 1 · About the Family or Group

This step helps us understand who this digital library is for. There are no right or wrong answers. You may keep things simple.

What we need in this step

Please prepare the following information. You may write it down on paper, in a text file, or use our template later.

  • Family or Group Name
    Example: “The Reyes Family”, “Dela Cruz Clan”, “St. Michael Community”
  • Project Type
    Family · Clan · Community · Organization
  • Main Contact Person
    Name and email address of the person we should communicate with
  • Preferred Output Size
    A5 · A4 · A3 · Book format · Not sure yet

If you are not sure

It is completely okay to write:

  • “Not sure yet”
  • “Please advise”
  • “We will decide later”

Many families start without complete details. We will help you refine this later.

Optional notes (if you want)

You may include any short notes such as:

  • This is a large family
  • This includes multiple branches
  • This is a community with sub-groups

These notes help us prepare the correct structure from the beginning.
