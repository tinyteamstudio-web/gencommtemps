Step 2: List People / Members

Start simple. A clean list of names is enough for now. You can refine details later through Living Updates.

What to list

  • Full name (or preferred display name)
  • Status (living or deceased — optional)
  • Short note (optional: nickname, adoption note, privacy request)

If you are unsure about spelling or dates, it’s okay. We can adjust later.

Simple list No uploads Email-based Can be updated later

Write it down (for your email later)

You can copy this format into your notes:

Person 1: Name — Status — Notes
Person 2: Name — Status — Notes
Person 3: Name — Status — Notes

Large families or communities are welcome. There is no limit.
