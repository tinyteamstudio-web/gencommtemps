Step 4: Send Your Email

This is the final step. Once we receive your email, we begin reviewing and building your library—carefully and manually.

What to include in your email

  • Payment receipt (screenshot or image)
  • Your name and product code (GEN-01 or GEN-02)
  • Library name (family or community name)
  • Your notes from Steps 1–3 (names, relationships, instructions)
  • Optional photos (only if you’re ready — this can wait)

Missing something? That’s okay. You can send what you have now and complete the rest later through Living Updates.

Email-only Manual review Privacy-respectful Updates allowed later
