Step 4 · Email Handoff (Final Step)

You’re almost done. This final step shows exactly how to send your details to us so we can prepare your TinyTeam Digital Library carefully and correctly.

What to send

Please gather the information from Steps 1–3. If you have photos, you may include them too.

  • Step 1: Family/Group name, project type, contact person, preferred output size
  • Step 2: People list (names, relationships, status)
  • Step 3: Special situations and private notes (optional)
  • Photos (optional): Any important family/community photos you want included

It is okay if you do not have everything yet. Start with what you have.

Where to send

Email: smallsteps@tinyteamstudio.online

Subject example: GEN-01 Submission – (Family Name) or GEN-02 Submission – (Community/Clan Name)

If you already paid

Please include your receipt (screenshot or reference number).

Payment verification is manual and human-reviewed.

Go to Pay via QR Open Thank You Page

What happens after you email

  1. We confirm receipt of your email
  2. We review your information carefully
  3. We may ask gentle clarification questions (if needed)
  4. We prepare your digital library structure
  5. We send delivery instructions or files via email

This is a human-handled process. We prioritize accuracy, respect, and long-term preservation.

Need help describing your family setup?

If your family or community structure is complicated, our Help Guide explains blended families, adoption, unknown parents, and memorial notes.
