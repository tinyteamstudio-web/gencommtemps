Step 3: Relationships & Special Setups

This step helps us build the structure correctly — especially for blended families, adoption, or multiple branches.

What to include (simple)

  • Parent → child relationships (who belongs to whom)
  • Spouse / partner relationships (if included)
  • Adoption / guardian notes (if applicable)
  • Any privacy requests (e.g., initials only)

You can write this in plain words. Example: “Ana is the daughter of Juan and Maria.”
