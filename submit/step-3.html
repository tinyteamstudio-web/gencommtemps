Step 3: Relationships & Special Setups

This step helps us build the structure correctly—especially for blended families, adoption/guardianship, multiple branches, or privacy-sensitive situations.

What to include

  • Parent → child connections (who belongs to whom)
  • Spouse/partner connections (if you want them shown)
  • Adoption / guardian notes (only what you are comfortable sharing)
  • Branch grouping (e.g., “Juan’s Line”, “Maria’s Line”)
  • Privacy requests (initials only, hide photos, remove details)

Plain words are fine. You do not need a perfect format.

Simple language Privacy-respectful Email-only Human-reviewed

Easy formats you can use

Choose any style below. One is enough:

  • Plain sentence: “Ana is the daughter of Juan and Maria.”
  • Arrow style: Juan + Maria → Ana
  • Notes style: “Mark is under Rose’s branch. Use initials only.”

If something is sensitive or complex, tell us your preferred display and privacy level. We will follow your instructions.
