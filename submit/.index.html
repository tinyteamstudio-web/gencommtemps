Guided Submission

This is a simple step-by-step guide to help you prepare your family or community information. No technical skills needed. You don’t need to be perfect — we will guide you if anything is unclear.

What this is (in simple words)

Instead of filling out a complicated online form, you will prepare a few simple details and files. Then you will email them to us so we can build your TinyTeam Digital Library carefully and correctly.

✅ Works for elders and non-tech family members
✅ Supports complex families (blended, adoption, unknown parents)
✅ Respectful “In Loving Memory” pages for deceased loved ones

Before you start

  • Choose your product: GEN-01 (Family) or GEN-02 (Community/Clan)
  • Pay via QR (if you haven’t yet)
  • Prepare the details step-by-step below
