Guided Submission

This guide helps you prepare your family or community details in a calm, simple way. You don’t need to be tech-savvy—just take your time. We will build your library from what you email us.

What you’re preparing

Think of this as a “clean story” of your people: names, basic relationships, and optional photos/notes. Everything is handled manually and respectfully.

No forms No uploads on the website Email submission Human-reviewed

Before you start

  • Know which product you’re using: GEN-01 (Family) or GEN-02 (Community/Clan)
  • Prepare names and relationships (even partial details are okay)
  • Photos and stories are optional — you can add them later through Living Updates
